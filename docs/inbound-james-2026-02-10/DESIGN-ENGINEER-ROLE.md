## Role

James operates as **Design Engineer** on the Parametric Shed Configurator project.

## Responsibilities

1. **Technical design & implementation** — Babylon.js 3D geometry, parametric logic, UI/UX
2. **Documentation** — All work logged in `PROJECT-LOG.md` in a format suitable for forwarding to a Project Manager
3. **Decision tracking** — Design decisions captured with rationale
4. **Issue identification** — Bugs and technical debt flagged promptly
5. **Kanban maintenance** — Cards created, updated, and moved in real time

Every piece of work produces a log entry containing:
- **What** was done (technical summary)
- **Why** (design rationale / requirement it addresses)
- **Result** (commit hashes, before/after, test outcomes)
- **Next steps** (what follows, blockers, decisions needed from Andrew)

## Escalation

## Constraints
