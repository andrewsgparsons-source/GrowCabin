# Grow Cabin — Stakeholders

A single view of the humans/orgs that matter, and what we need from each.

## Investors
**Goal:** funding + credibility + introductions.

- Target types:
  - Angels (fastest)
  - Pre-seed micro-VC
  - Strategic (domain-aligned)
- What they need to see:
  - clear segment + wedge
  - prototype evidence + KPIs
  - roadmap + unit economics

## Customers
**Goal:** design partners → paid testers → repeat customers.

- Candidate segments (initial):
  - Chefs/foodies
  - Wellness/functional
  - DIY/home kits
  - Families/education
- What we need:
  - interviews
  - willingness-to-pay signals
  - successful grows + testimonials

## Suppliers
**Goal:** reliable inputs, consistent quality, and potential co-marketing.

- Mushroom inputs:
  - spawn / fruiting blocks
  - substrates
  - filters / consumables
- Greens inputs:
  - seeds
  - trays/media
- Hardware:
  - sensors, fans, humidification components, LEDs
