_Last updated: 2026-02-11_

## Options

- Partnership
- Private Limited Company (Ltd)

## Scoring criteria (1–5)
- Investor readiness
- Liability protection
- Setup simplicity/speed (simpler = higher)
- Admin/compliance overhead (lower = higher)
- Brand/commercial credibility
